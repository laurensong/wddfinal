About the Band

LANY is a three piece alternative band originated from Los Angeles, California with their name, LANY, an acronym for "Los Angeles New York." In March 2014, it was formed by Paul Klein, who previously had a solo career, and then met up with Jake Goss and Les Priest for the formation of LANY. In 2015, they embarked on American tours opening for artists such as Troye Sivan, Ellie Goulding, and Halsey. They have released many famous singles such as "ILYSB" and began their second headline tour in the fall of 2016 after releasing their album. Their EPs are chronologically named "I Loved You."
